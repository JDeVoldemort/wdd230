Target Audience

  • Who: Local business owners, local leaders, and other community members
  • Age: 12 to 100
  • Income: All Income levels included
  • Motivation: Improve distribution of knowledge of our community's history and ongoing story. Seek to get more involvement, and promote local business.

Persona 1: Jeramiah Puddlefoot

  • Occupation: Runs local Newspaper
  • Demographics: 49 years old. Married, Father of 4, Bachelors in Creative Writing
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: Hopes to keep up on local events and businesses for his newspaper.
  • Social: Likes hiking in the woods with family, plays golf on weekends, tries to keep his mother up to date on local events.
  • Technology: up to date on current trends. Uses a desktop at work, and mostly his phone at home.
  • Quote: "Nobody makes time for the newspaper anymore!"

Persona 2: Stan Grunkle

  • Occupation: Local tourist shop owner
  • Demographics and Education: 69 years old. Bachelors in Performing Arts.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: He just discovered the GFC of Commerce has a website and wants to see his shop is represented.
  • Social: A Grouchy individual with occasional visiting relatives. He has been a fixture in this town for a long time.
  • Technology: Rarely uses technology, but recently got a dated laptop and a smartphone.
  • Quote: "What is a text message?"
